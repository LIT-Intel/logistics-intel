# LIT — Claude Design video creative briefs
**Date:** 2026-05-11 · **Owner:** Marketing / GTM · **Audience:** Claude Design (vector animation team)

## Brand notes that apply to every video

- **Palette.** Dark navy background (`#0F172A` → `#1E293B` gradient). Brand blue accent `#2563EB`. Cyan signal accent `#00F0FF` (used sparingly for emphasis). Off-white text `#F8FAFC` on dark; `#0F172A` on light.
- **Typography.** Inter (or system equivalent). Headlines tight tracking, ~`-0.02em`. Body text neutral, no caps unless quoting product labels.
- **Logo lockup.** "Logistic Intel" with "Intel" in `#2563EB`. Always pair with the LIT mark.
- **Motion style.** Calm, confident, data-led. No bouncy easing. Linear or `easeOutCubic`. Element entries should feel like a Bloomberg terminal, not a Saas demo reel.
- **Aspect deliverables per video.** 16:9 (1920×1080, web hero / paid), 9:16 (1080×1920, TikTok / Reels / Shorts), 1:1 (1080×1080, LinkedIn feed).
- **Length.** 15 seconds each, ±1s.
- **Sound.** Optional underscore. All copy on-screen — assume no audio.
- **End frame.** Last 2 seconds: LIT logo + one clear CTA + URL.
- **Sources of imagery.** Real Pulse Brief modal, real importer profile cards, real lane visualizations. Never fabricated UI.

## 1) Freight Leads

**Title:** *Find shippers actually shipping.*
**Objective:** Drive `/freight-leads` traffic for the "freight leads" SEO bucket. Position LIT as shipment-intelligence-backed lead generation, not a contact database.
**Target CTA:** Start free trial → `app.logisticintel.com/signup`
**Visual direction:** Animated transition from a generic CSV list (gray rows, low fidelity) into the LIT importer directory cards with real logos and TEU stats. End on a single highlighted Samsung Electronics card with its KPI strip animating in.
**On-screen text (timed):**
- 0-3s: "Stop buying stale lead lists."
- 3-7s: "26,787 active US importers."
- 7-11s: "Live from US Customs filings."
- 11-15s: "logisticintel.com/freight-leads" + LIT mark
**Motion style:** Cards stagger-in left to right, KPI numbers count up over 600ms each.
**Aspect notes:**
- 16:9 — full directory grid (3 cols → 1 highlighted)
- 9:16 — stacked single card with KPI strip dominating
- 1:1 — 2-row grid resolving to one centered card
